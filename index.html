The Band

Ongoing work as lead designer for the Huddersfield born band that have played together for over 10 years. The band fall under the genre of indie music, but have folk & rock influences that are more apparent in their later albums.

The Project

This project features the work from the band's latest album "making waves".

The new direction is vastly different from previous albums in the sense that the direction is more vibrant & loud to reflect the style of their music now.
