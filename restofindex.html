Meet Our Team

We have carefully cultivated a small team of gifted and diverse individuals, all of whom are industry superstars. By being extremely selective about our hires, we are able to invest a substantial amount of resources to attract and retain top performers.

Join Us
Martin Ditto President
Martin is chief executive and leads all development activities at Ditto Residential. He is responsible for sourcing acquisitions and investment opportunities, as well as overseeing design direction and growth strategy. He previously worked in development at Bozzuto and Monument Realty. Martin has a degree in Economics from Vanderbilt University and holds a Masters of Science in Real Estate from the Carey Business School at Johns Hopkins University.
martin@dittodc.com
Brian BurkeCFO
Brian manages all acquisition, finance and business operations at Ditto Residential. He is responsible for long and short-term financing, capitalization and financial strategy. Brian was previously a Senior Director of Corporate Development at Capital One and an Investment Banking Analyst at Bank of America Securities. Brian has a degree in Economics from Stanford University and holds an M.B.A. from the Haas School of Business at UC Berkeley.
brian@dittodc.com
Callie BruemmerMarketing
Callie is responsible for all communication and marketing activities at Ditto Residential. She directs brand strategy, public relations and events. Callie has a degree in Strategic Communication and International Studies from the School of Journalism and Mass Communication at the University of Wisconsin-Madison.
callie@dittodc.com
Chris KoellikerVP Construction
Chris leads all construction operations at Ditto Residential. He oversees a team of project managers and field staff and is responsible for maintaining our high standards of construction. Chris previously worked in both residential and commercial construction, including starting his own homebuilding company. Chris has a Master of Science in Building Construction from Auburn University and is a member of the Design-Build Institute of America.
chris@dittodc.com
Dan RothDevelopment
Dan manages development for our multifamily assets. He was previously a Development Consultant at Metropolitan Partnership after 8 years in management and process consulting with Protiviti Inc. Dan has a degree in Operations Management and Computer Information Systems from Indiana University and holds an M.B.A. in Real Estate and Urban Development from George Washington University.
dan@dittodc.com
Keyur ShahProject Manager
Keyur oversees predevelopment, design and construction of our development projects. He was previously a project manager at Revolution Recovery after a tour in Kenya leading a microfinance and professional development corporation. He started his career as an engineer at Clark Construction. Keyur has a degree in Architecture from the University of Virginia.
keyur@dittodc.com
Stephanie ErwinOperations
Stephanie is responsible for asset management and predevelopment activities. She manages all municipal relations, utility coordination and early stage project management of our development projects. Stephanie has a degree in Management Information Systems and Finance from Washington University and is currently working towards a Master of Science in Real Estate Development at New York University.
stephanie@dittodc.com
Tim MansonProject Manager
Tim oversees predevelopment, design and construction of our development projects. Tim was previously a superintendent at Clark Construction where he was responsible for multi-family construction at both the CityCenterDC and Square 54/The Avenue developments.
